Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. What Data We Collect

  • Account details (username, password)
  • Contact details (if applicable)
  • Claim data (including accident details, demographics, injury info)
  • Usage logs and submitted feedback

2. Why We Collect Your Data

We collect data to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process claims and generate invoices
  • Monitor system usage for security and optimization
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on:

  • Contractual necessity
  • Legal obligation
  • Legitimate interest
  • Your explicit consent (for sensitive data)

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers and protected through encryption, access control, and secure communication (HTTPS).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Rectify incorrect information
  • Delete your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Download a copy of your data (portability)

6. Contact

If you have any questions or want to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at support@example.com.
